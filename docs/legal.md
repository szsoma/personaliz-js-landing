For a simple “notify me when it launches” landing page, your privacy policy can be straightforward. You don’t need a long, enterprise-style document, but you should clearly explain what data you collect, why you collect it, which services process it, and users’ rights (especially if you’re collecting emails from EU visitors).

Here’s a version you can publish with only a few placeholders to customize.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit this website and subscribe to receive updates about the launch of the product.

We respect your privacy and only collect the information necessary to operate this website and send launch-related emails.

⸻

Information We Collect

If you subscribe to receive updates, we collect:

* Your email address
* The date and time of your subscription

Depending on your browser and device, some technical information may also be collected automatically, such as:

* IP address
* Browser type
* Device information
* Pages visited
* Referral URL

This information may be collected by our hosting providers or other third-party services used to operate the website.

⸻

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

* Send product launch announcements
* Share important updates about the project
* Notify you when new versions become available
* Respond to questions if you contact us

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not use your email address for unrelated marketing without your consent.

⸻

Email Subscriptions

Email subscriptions are managed through Kit (formerly ConvertKit).

When you submit the subscription form, your email address is securely processed and stored by Kit.

You can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link included in every email.

⸻

Third-Party Services

We use the following service providers to operate this website:

Vercel

This website is hosted by Vercel.

Vercel may process technical information such as IP addresses, browser information, request logs, and performance data necessary to deliver the website securely.

Cloudflare

The personalization script and related assets may be delivered through Cloudflare Workers.

Cloudflare may process technical information required to securely deliver content, improve performance, and protect against abuse.

Kit

Kit is used to manage email subscriptions and send launch updates.

When you subscribe, your email address is processed according to Kit’s privacy practices.

⸻

Cookies and Local Storage

The launch website itself does not intentionally use cookies for marketing purposes.

If functionality is added in the future that requires cookies or browser storage, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

⸻

Data Retention

We keep your email address for as long as you remain subscribed.

If you unsubscribe, your information will be removed or retained only as necessary to comply with legal obligations or maintain suppression lists that prevent future unwanted emails.

⸻

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:

* Access your personal data
* Correct inaccurate information
* Request deletion of your data
* Withdraw your consent
* Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the email address below.

⸻

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

⸻

International Data Transfers

Some of the third-party services used to operate this website may process information outside your country of residence.

These providers implement appropriate safeguards for international data transfers where required by applicable law.

⸻

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

The latest version will always be available on this page with the updated revision date.

⸻

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact:

Email: your@email.com
